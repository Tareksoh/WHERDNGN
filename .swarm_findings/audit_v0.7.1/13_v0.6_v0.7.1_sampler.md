# Audit: v0.7.1 B-97 + v0.6.1 sampler biases

HEAD = v0.7.2. Scope: B-97 opp-meld suit avoidance (Bot.lua:1579-1607)
plus v0.6.1 sampler biases (BotMaster.lua:97-129, 398-418).

## Bias correctness

**B-97 opp-meld inference source.** `Bot.lua:1588-1606` reads
`S.s.meldsByTeam[oppTeam]` directly, NOT `OnPlayObserved` / play
history. Inference is structural: declared melds are authoritative
(the run is a known holding, not a guess), so this is the correct
source. Filters: `m.kind:sub(1,3) == "seq"` excludes carrés (right —
4-of-a-rank doesn't imply suit-lead intent), and `m.suit ~= contract.trump`
excludes trump suit (right — non-trump lead selection only).

**Sun-bidder partner concentration.** `getPartnerCards` at
`BotMaster.lua:121-127` writes `desire["A"..s] = 8` and
`desire["K"..s] = 4` for all four suits when `contract.type ==
K.BID_SUN`. Pre-v0.6.1 returned `{}` — confirmed gap.

## Activation gating

**B-97**: `Bot.IsM3lm() and not isBidderTeam` at line 1588 — correct
M3lm gate (per CHANGELOG note "M3lm-gated since it relies on
accumulated meld observations") and only fires on defender-side leads,
matching design intent.

**v0.6.1 biases**: `sampleConsistentDeal` is only invoked from
`BotMaster.PickPlay`, which is gated by `BM.IsActive()` =
`saudiMasterBots == true` (BotMaster.lua:131-134). Saudi Master
strictly extends Fzloky/M3lm, so M3lm+ requirement holds (in fact
tighter than M3lm — Saudi-Master-only).

## Double-counting

**leadCount vs. Fzloky/firstDiscard suit-bias**: leadCount writes
`desire[suit] = 1` only **for opponent seats** (`sIsOpponent` at
line 411) and only **if `not desire[suit]` already set** (line 414).
Fzloky pSignalSuit logic applies to **partner**, not opponents — no
collision. `likelyKawesh`-clear precedes leadCount (line 412 guard),
so Kawesh-cleared opps stay cleared. Clean.

**dumpHighSeen vs. leadCount**: dumpHighSeen does not appear in the
sampler — no overlap path.

**Sun-bidder partner Aces (8) vs. defender Aces (8)**: same weight;
defenderDesire and partnerDesire apply to **disjoint seats** (defender
= opp team, partner = bidder's partner). No double-count.

## Section 11 alignment

Section 11 row "Partner is Sun bidder → assume one long suit (4+) with
concentrated high cards. Common / 02. Sampler bias (not yet wired)" —
the v0.6.1 wire encodes the **highs concentration** (A=8/K=4 across
all suits). The "one long suit (4+)" half is intentionally NOT encoded
(comment at BotMaster.lua:114-116 acknowledges this — length emerges
from random fill since the source video doesn't localize the suit).
Partial implementation, but documented.

## Verdict

**PASS.** B-97 uses the correct authoritative inference source;
v0.6.1 biases activate at the right tier (Saudi Master, ⊂ M3lm+);
weights don't double-count across leadCount/Fzloky/defender desire;
and Section 11 rule 4 alignment is honest about its partial scope.
No functional defects found. Tests: 226/226 (v0.6.1), 291/291 (v0.7.1).
