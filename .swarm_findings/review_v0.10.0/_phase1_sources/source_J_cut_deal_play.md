## B. Cut and deal procedure (#39)

### J-009 — Dealer selected by lot OR loser-deals rule
- **Source:** #39 @ 00:21-00:36
- **Arabic (≤15):** "البلوت النازل هو اللي يوزع"
- **English:** First session: dealer chosen by lot/agreement. After: the losing team from prior session deals (the "descender" deals).
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Pre-deal

### J-010 — Cutter is the player on dealer's left
- **Source:** #39 @ 00:45-00:53
- **Arabic (≤15):** "اللي على يسارك … قص … فاليسار دائما يقص"
- **English:** Before dealing, the dealer asks the player on their left to cut ("qass"). Left always cuts.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Pre-deal

### J-011 — Multiple legal cut shapes
- **Source:** #39 @ 00:53-01:48
- **Arabic (≤15):** "القص له اشكال كثيره"
- **English:** Several legal cut shapes: (a) split deck and place in middle; (b) lift the bottom card and reveal; (c) shuffle ("khabsah") — sessions vary on whether 1 or up to 3 shuffles allowed; (d) take last 2-3 cards; (e) give cut cards to partner. Cuts are taken from BOTTOM only, never top.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Cut

### J-012 — Cutter may decline ("rooh")
- **Source:** #39 @ 01:48-01:55
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ممكن يقول لك روح … خلاص روح ما يبغى يقص"
- **English:** Cutter may say "rooh" (go ahead) and decline to cut.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Cut

### J-013 — Failure to ask for cut: opponent decides
- **Source:** #39 @ 01:55-02:04
- **Arabic (≤15):** "في حاله انك ما سالته بدات توزع … سعيد التوزيع"
- **English:** If dealer forgets to ask for the cut and starts dealing, then if opponent says "you didn't ask me," the deal stands ("سعيد التوزيع" — happy/blessed deal). Otherwise can be challenged.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Pre-deal `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-014 — Deal direction: counter-clockwise from dealer's right
- **Source:** #39 @ 02:04-02:13
- **Arabic (≤15):** "عكس عقارب الساعه من اليمين"
- **English:** Deal counter-clockwise. First card goes to player on dealer's RIGHT (this player has priority/eldest hand for buying).
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Deal

### J-015 — Deal pattern (canonical): 3-3-2 + show + 2-2-3 (post-buy)
- **Source:** #39 @ 02:13-02:20, 02:21-02:24, 04:00-04:23
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ثلاثه ثلاثه … اثنين اثنين اثنين … ورقه مكشوفه"
- **English:** Phase 1 deal: 3 cards each player, then 2 each (= 5 each), then ONE FACE-UP card to start the auction. After buy: dealer adds 2 more to the buyer (replacing the face-up) and 3 more to each other player → 8 total each.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Deal `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-016 — Deal pattern variant: 2-2-3
- **Source:** #39 @ 02:31-02:48
- **Arabic (≤15):** "بدل ما يبدو يرجعوا ثلاثه … يوزع اثنين اثنين"
- **English:** Some sessions invert: 2-2 first, then 3 (still totals 5 + face-up). Some sessions allow this, others forbid; convention varies.
- **Confidence:** High (explicitly noted as variant)
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Deal

### J-017 — Bid-card (face-up) is mandatory
- **Source:** #39 @ 02:24-02:31, 03:20-03:27
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ورقه مكشوفه اللي هيشتغل عليها لازم"

### J-018 — No peeking at others' cards

### J-023 — Final hand size: 8 cards each

## C. Cut/deal errors (#40) — kasho/qaid/maashi triad

### J-024 — Three penalty options for opponent on any error
- **Source:** #40 @ 00:25-00:39
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ثلاثه خيارات فاما عقوبه شديده … خفيفه يا اما يمشي"
- **English:** When ANY error occurs, opposing team has THREE choices: (1) **Qaid** (severe penalty — claim full points), (2) **Kasho/Takwish** (light penalty — redeal), (3) **Mashi** (let play continue / agree privately).
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-025 — Qaid penalty value
- **Source:** #40 @ 00:39-00:50
- **Arabic (≤15):** "النقاط كامله حق الجيم … لو الصن 26 … الحكم 16"
- **English:** Qaid awards the full contract value to the wronged team: Sun = 26 points, Hokm = 16 points (no project bonuses unless attached). Penalized side scores zero.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling
- **Thresholds:** Sun qaid = 26, Hokm qaid = 16 `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-026 — Kasho/Takwish ≈ "Aajab/Ma-Aajab" mechanism
- **Source:** #40 @ 00:50-01:02, 09:08-10:00
- **Arabic (≤15):** "اللي هي التكويش او الكوشه … عجب ولا ما عجب"
- **English:** Light penalty = "Kasho" or "Aajab/Ma-Aajab" ("you like? you don't?"): one player asks partner if they like the hand; if both decline, it's a redeal with no scoring.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-027 — Mashi (let it ride)
- **Source:** #40 @ 01:02-01:11
- **Arabic (≤15):** "تمشي اللعب خلاص … ما حد يرمي الورق"
- **English:** Third option: just continue play. Most important: nobody throws the cards down — play must continue.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling

### J-028 — Two main deal-stage errors
- **Source:** #40 @ 01:15-01:31
- **Arabic (≤15):** "يا اما توزع غلط … او اذا ورق انكشفت بالغلط"
- **English:** Two main errors during cut/deal: (a) wrong card count to a player (over- or under-deal); (b) a card accidentally exposed during the deal.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Deal `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-029 — Penalty severity is session-dependent
- **Source:** #40 @ 01:34-01:53, 02:00-02:07
- **Arabic (≤15):** "تختلف من جلسه لجلسه"
- **English:** Penalty selection is house/session convention; "the cleverest in the world is wrong by ten" ("الشاطر بعشره"). Final say is opponent's choice.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling

### J-030 — Penalty severity scales with game state
- **Source:** #40 @ 02:11-02:30
- **Arabic (≤15):** "اذا كان الجيم حامي … يا فريق يفوز يا فريق الاخر"
- **English:** If the game is "hot" (close to ending), don't let it ride; choose Kasho or Qaid since one error could decide the match.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Error-handling `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-031 — Misdeal, missing card: deficit-fix flow
- **Source:** #40 @ 03:25-04:13
- **Arabic (≤15):** "خصمك عنده خيارين طبعا ما يقيد"
- **English:** If a player got too few cards AND face-up card was already revealed: opponent has two options (Kasho or Mashi); cannot Qaid (because face-up is on the table). Mashi flow: dealer gives the next undealt card to the under-dealt player.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Short hand
- **Phase:** Deal `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-032 — Misdeal, extra card given: opponent's choice
- **Source:** #40 @ 04:16-04:46, 05:23-06:01
- **Arabic (≤15):** "من حقه انه يكوش او ينزل ثالث ورق"
- **English:** If a player got an extra card: opponent may Kasho OR have them play down the 3rd card (the misdealt extra). Dealer continues dealing as if normal.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Long hand
- **Phase:** Deal `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-033 — Misdeal AFTER buy = Tawzee Qaid (16 points)
- **Source:** #40 @ 06:09-06:48
- **Arabic (≤15):** "التوزيع ب 16 نقطه نفس نقاط الحكم"
- **English:** If misdeal discovered AFTER buyer bought (e.g., 9 cards or 7 cards): Qaid = "Tawzee Qaid" = 16 points (same as Hokm value). Author argues this is correct over "Sun Qaid" because projects (mashroo) might already be in play.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Post-buy
- **Thresholds:** Tawzee Qaid = 16 `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-034 — Project bonuses ARE counted toward the wronged side's Qaid total
- **Source:** #40 @ 06:33-06:48
- **Arabic (≤15):** "اذا كان عنده مشروع ينحسب في القلو"
- **English:** When taking Qaid, if the wronged side has a project (Sera, Carré, etc.), it adds to their Qaid total. So Qaid includes contract value + projects of the wronged side.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Project in hand
- **Phase:** Scoring `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-035 — Mashi + extra-card swap: pick "uncut" suit
- **Source:** #40 @ 06:53-07:38
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ياخذ لون غير مقطوع من الخصم"
- **English:** When choosing Mashi to swap an extra card from over-dealt player, the correct rule is to take a NON-VOID suit from the over-dealt player (a suit they have ≥ 2 of). Taking their singleton would help them by creating a void.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Suit-distribution dependent
- **Phase:** Error-fix `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-036 — Exposed-card rule: source of the error matters
- **Source:** #40 @ 08:30-09:08
- **Arabic (≤15):** "يفرق مرحله ما قبل الشراء ويفرق مرحله الشراء"

### J-037 — Exposed card: Aajab/Ma-Aajab option
- **Source:** #40 @ 09:11-09:55
- **Arabic (≤15):** "خيارين يا اما عجب ولا ما عجب اللي هي تقريبا نفس التكويش"

## D. Sun (no-trump) play basics (#41)

### J-042 — Two universal rules for Sun
- **Source:** #41 @ 00:32-00:38
- **Arabic (≤15):** "الكبير ياكل الصغير … لازم تلعب نفس شكل الارض"
- **English:** Two simple Sun rules: (1) high card wins; (2) MUST follow suit if able ("must-play same shape as ground"). Author calls this "two simple things."
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Trick play (Sun) `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-043 — Sort hand alternating colors
- **Source:** #41 @ 00:38-00:47
- **Arabic (≤15):** "ترتب اوراقك … احمر اسود احمر اسود"
- **English:** Best practice: arrange hand alternating red-black-red-black, large to small within suits. Helps spot projects and avoid mistakes.
- **Confidence:** Medium (advice, not rule)
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Pre-play

### J-044 — Q before 10 in project (sera) ordering
- **Source:** #41 @ 00:47-00:51
- **Arabic (≤15):** "في ترتيب السره البنت قبل العشره"
- **English:** In project (sera/sequence) ordering: Q before 10 (using natural rank). NOT the Sun trick-rank order. Distinction: trick rank is K > Q > 10 — but FOR PROJECTS the natural order J-Q-K is used.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Sequential cards
- **Phase:** Project declaration `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-045 — Eldest hand (right of dealer) leads first trick
- **Source:** #41 @ 01:05-01:09
- **Arabic (≤15):** "هذا الشخص هو على اليمين المزع هو اللي راح يبدا"
- **English:** Player on dealer's right leads the opening trick.
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** N/A
- **Phase:** Opening lead `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-046 — Project announcement timing: declare on trick 1, show on trick 2
- **Source:** #41 @ 01:22-01:34
- **Arabic (≤15):** "في اول دور او اول اكله وثاني اكله بينزل المشروع"
- **English:** A player with a project says the project name on trick 1; lays the project cards down on trick 2. If they play before declaring, the project is forfeited ("ma yu'tabar").
- **Confidence:** High
- **Hand-shape:** Has project
- **Phase:** Trick 1 / Trick 2 `**[FOCUS]**`

### J-047 — "Sera" / "Sera Malaki" naming

### J-050 — Void → discard freely (Sun)

### J-051 — Void-discard does NOT win the trick (Sun)
