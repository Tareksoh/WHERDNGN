### A12. Belote declaration timing — split, two-part, NOT at trick-1

**[FOCUS]** — relevant cluster (declaration timing).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Source | #32 lines ~377–407 |
| Arabic ≤15 | "البلوت ما ينقال الا لما تلعب ثاني ورقه" |
| English | "Belote is NOT spoken UNTIL you play the second card (of the pair)" |
| Confidence | HIGH |
| Hand-shape | Trump K+Q in your hand |
| Phase | Mid-trick declaration |
| Thresholds | Played in TWO parts: say "بلوت" when you play the K (or Q) the second time |

**Critical mechanic**: Speaker walks through example. You announce other melds (سره/خمسين/ميه) BEFORE playing your first card on trick 1. Belote is **different** — you do NOT pre-announce it like the others.

**Step 1** (#32 line ~395): when you play the FIRST trump face card (e.g. Q of trump as a follow), say nothing — or just say it as you play it.

**Step 2** (#32 line ~399): when you play the SECOND trump face card (the partner card, completing the K+Q pair), THAT is when you say "بلوت".

**Alternative timing** (#32 line ~404): you can say "معايا بلوت" at SCORING time at the end of the round, after all tricks are done. Speaker treats both as valid.

**Restriction noted** (#32 line ~467): "البلوت يجي مع ميه سوق انتبه" — Belote canNOT be combined with a sequence meld declaration in pre-play unless trumps are part of the sequence (the شايب-وبنت are within your tierce/quarte).
