**Verdict — CATEGORICAL preference applied within Bayesian framework.**

- The **Tahreeb-priority RULE** is **categorical** (دائما — always). When both Tahreeb and Tanfeer interpretations are syntactically possible, Tahreeb wins by hard rule.

**Implementation note:** Code should NOT independently combine Tahreeb and Tanfeer probabilities via additive Bayes. It must apply Tahreeb-priority gating as a hard switch (cancel Tanfeer when Tahreeb is plausible), THEN update remaining Bayesian priors per-trick.

**Confidence: HIGH** for the categorical-rule layer; HIGH for the Bayesian-prior layer; MEDIUM for the synthesis interpretation (made explicit in A-Src-19 Section 6).
