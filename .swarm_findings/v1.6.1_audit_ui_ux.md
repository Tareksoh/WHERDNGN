# v1.6.1 UI/UX Audit

Scope: visual hierarchy, interaction responsiveness, click-through,
banner/glow timing, error feedback, resize, frame strata, tooltips,
color, animation. Sources read end-to-end: `UI.lua` (3845 lines),
`MinimapIcon.lua`, `Easter.lua`, relevant slices of `Constants.lua`,
`State.lua`, `Net.lua`. Cross-checked against CHANGELOG.md history
to skip already-fixed items (dice banner, R1-Sun gate, deny-SWA
confirm, BALOOT button label, banner-title overflow, etc.).

## TL;DR

- Total issues: 25 (1 CRITICAL, 6 HIGH, 11 MED, 7 LOW)
- Severity breakdown: 1 CRIT / 6 HIGH / 11 MED / 7 LOW
- Top 3 most-impactful fixes:
  1. UX-13 [CRIT]: BALOOT! pulse OnUpdate is never cleared on
     button pool reuse — pooled action button keeps animating its
     fontstring color in subsequent phases, recoloring unrelated
     buttons. UI.lua:2152 vs clearActions UI.lua:1664.
  2. UX-04 [HIGH]: Action buttons are fixed 90px wide; armed-
     confirm labels ("Confirm Triple x3 (closed)?", deny-SWA's
     ~150-char string) overflow / truncate hard. UI.lua:1676,
     1869-1871, 2081-2083.
  3. UX-21 [HIGH]: Hand-card OnClick has no `S.s.paused` check —
     during pause the cards still receive clicks; rejection
     happens silently in Net.lua. User clicks → nothing visibly
     happens. UI.lua:2330.

## Visual hierarchy

### UX-01: Top-bar icon row competes with title and is asymmetric
- Severity: MED
- Cue: top-row left half = Sound checkbox + Scale −/+ buttons; top-
  centre = "Loot & Baloot" title with subtitle "(KZKZ will come)";
  top-right = game-id, close. The title sits between functional
  controls; on a narrow scale (0.7) the subtitle drifts close to
  the +/− buttons (subtitle is anchored to title's RIGHT edge,
  title is centered → positions are scale-independent but the
  "−/+" sit at TOPLEFT 96 absolute, so at scale 0.7 they end up
  "inside" the title region).
- Root cause: UI.lua:441-444 (scale buttons absolute-anchored at
  TOPLEFT, 96, -8) vs UI.lua:421-433 (title anchored to TOP, 0,
  -10).
- Fix sketch: anchor scale +/− to muteBtn's RIGHT instead of
  TOPLEFT 96, so the cluster grows/shrinks together. Or move
  the "− +" group to the bottom-right next to the Min toggle so
  the top stays clean.

### UX-02: Bid card and active-trick cards share the same 5 anchored slots, with reuse not visually obvious
- Severity: MED
- Cue: during bidding phases the bid card sits at center 0,0
  (`centerCards.bid`). When PLAY starts, that slot is hidden and
  4 cardinal slots (`bottom`, `top`, `left`, `right`) take over.
  The transition is abrupt (no fade); the bid card disappears
  the instant phase switches.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1620-1625 (slot construction); UI.lua:2667-
  2752 renderCenter has no transition between bid-display and
  trick-display states.
- Fix sketch: brief alpha fade of `centerCards.bid` over ~150ms
  before clearing, matching the existing `animateLand` cadence
  for incoming cards.

### UX-03: Bottom local-bar meld strip overlays name+meld text simultaneously
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: the local meld strip is anchored INSIDE the localBar with
  `BOTTOM, localBar, BOTTOM, 0, 0` (UI.lua:1609). When melds are
  declared, the strip covers the player's own name + dealer
  prefix.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1601-1609. The comment acknowledges this
  ("overlays the localBar's name/score text — acceptable since
  melds and per-seat score never need to be read together") but
  the dealer-D prefix and the local-name are still useful
  context, especially in 4-human games where you want to confirm
  it's still your meld.
- Fix sketch: put the strip ABOVE the localBar (anchored
  `BOTTOM, localBar, TOP, 0, 2`) instead of inside it. Bumps up
  by ~38px during 5-second window only.

### UX-23: SWA pending banner overlaps the top trick card slot
- Severity: MED
- Cue: SWA banner anchored `TOP, centerPad, TOP, 0, -32`, height
  100, FrameLevel +50 — geometrically sits at y +83..-17 from
  centerPad center. The top center-card slot is at y +58 (height
  90 → +13..+103). Banner overlaps the partner's just-played
  card during a mid-trick SWA call. SWA can fire mid-trick
  (after partner played but before trick resolves).
- Root cause: UI.lua:1411-1413, 1416 vs UI.lua:1621.
- Fix sketch: increase the SWA banner top inset to clear the top
  card slot (e.g. anchor to TOP at -110 px with banner.cardSlots
  re-anchored), OR fade the partner's top-trick card to 0.4 alpha
  while SWA banner is visible.

## Button / interaction responsiveness

### UX-04: Fixed-width action buttons truncate armed-confirm labels [HIGH]
- Severity: HIGH
- Cue: action buttons are 90px wide. Armed labels include
  "|cffff5555Confirm Triple x3 (closed)?|r" (~30 visible chars),
  "Confirm Four & close?" (~22 chars), and the deny-SWA ~150-
  char "Confirm Deny — caller's invalid claim costs them ~30 pts;
  if Deny is wrong, your team takes the penalty.|r". WoW Button
  text doesn't auto-fit — it left-clips. User can't read the
  prompt that they were just shown.
- Root cause: makeButton at UI.lua:378-387 (fixed `b:SetSize(w
  or 80, h or 22)`); addAction at UI.lua:1676; armed labels at
  UI.lua:1846-1851, 1866-1871, 1882-1887, 1896-1898, 2055-2057,
  2081-2083.
- Fix sketch: when armed label exceeds normal-label width, render
  it as a tooltip line / banner above the button rather than
  inline. Or grow the action button width based on `:GetText()`'s
  measured pixel size when armed.

### UX-05: BALOOT! button has 2 Hz pulsing yellow even when nothing demands urgency
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: the K+Q-of-trump announce button pulses constantly between
  gold and bright yellow at 2 Hz the entire time it's visible
  (until the player either clicks it or no longer holds the pair
  — which can be many tricks). Eye-grab is justified for the
  first ~5 seconds, but persistent flashing fatigues attention
  and competes with the gold turn-glow on the active seat.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2152-2163 (no time decay).
- Fix sketch: pulse for the first 4 seconds after appearance,
  then settle to a static bright yellow.

### UX-06: BALOOT! pulse never fires Stop on partial visibility / pool reuse
- Severity: see UX-13 below — same issue, primary impact catalogued there.

### UX-07: SWA-vote Accept button is single-click while Deny is two-click
- Severity: MED
- Cue: per UI.lua:2079-2083, Accept is a one-click `addAction`,
  Deny is a `addConfirmAction`. The justification is sound (Accept
  is benign for the responder, Deny risks costing your team) but
  the asymmetry violates user expectation of "comparable buttons,
  comparable confirmation". A misclick on Accept is harmless to
  the responder's team but actually rewards a bot/AKA-deceptive
  caller — a single-click bot SWA in a tight situation may auto-
  approve before the partner can think.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2079 (Accept = addAction).
- Fix sketch: arm-and-fire pattern for Accept, with a 0.5s window
  (lighter than Deny's full 2s). Or change the layout so the
  more-stakes button sits leftmost.

### UX-08: Card-hand hover lift is unpredictable on legal vs illegal cards
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: cards lift 6px on hover only when `isPlayable` (UI.lua:
  2319-2323). But all 8 cards are clickable (Saudi Takweesh
  rule allows illegal play), so hovering an illegal card has no
  feedback while still being click-actionable. Players reasonably
  read "no hover lift = not clickable" and may skip the card —
  reducing the educational pressure of Takweesh.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2317-2323 (hover gated on isPlayable, no
  legal-vs-illegal differentiation).
- Fix sketch: lift 6px on legal, 3px on illegal; or different
  hover-tint colors keyed to legalSet membership.

### UX-13: BALOOT! pulse OnUpdate is never cleared on action-pool reuse [CRITICAL]
- Severity: CRITICAL
- Cue: when BALOOT! is shown, an OnUpdate ticker is bound to the
  pooled action button (UI.lua:2152-2163). Subsequent renders
  call `clearActions()` which only resets OnClick (UI.lua:1664-
  1670) — OnUpdate stays. The pulser keeps fighting `SetText` and
  `SetTextColor` from later phases (Pass, Hokm, Skip, Takweesh,
  …): the reused button's label flashes gold-yellow regardless of
  the current label/color. After 30+ seconds the visuals desync
  noticeably; cumulative across a long match the issue compounds
  because `actionPool[i]` slots are stable.
- Root cause: clearActions at UI.lua:1664 doesn't `SetScript(
  "OnUpdate", nil)`; addAction at UI.lua:1672 doesn't reset
  OnUpdate either. The pulse install at UI.lua:2152-2163 is the
  only place the script is set, and it's set on whatever pooled
  button addAction returned at that index.
- Fix sketch: in `clearActions`, also reset OnUpdate
  (`b:SetScript("OnUpdate", nil)`) AND reset the fontstring
  text-color back to the white default so any leftover tint
  clears.

## Click-through and frame-strata

### UX-21: Hand cards remain clickable while paused (silent rejection) [HIGH]
- Severity: HIGH
- Cue: when the host pauses, the trick area shows a "PAUSED"
  overlay. The hand row sits OUTSIDE centerPad in tablePanel.
  Clicking a card fires `LocalPlay` which silently no-ops in
  Net.lua's pause guard. User sees the card highlight but the
  trick doesn't update. No error feedback.
- Root cause: renderHand OnClick at UI.lua:2330 has no pause
  check; the pauseOverlay at UI.lua:1290-1301 is parented to
  `centerPad` so it doesn't cover handRow.
- Fix sketch: gate the handRow's OnClick on `S.s.paused == false`
  AND/OR show a transient toast "Paused — host must resume" when
  a card is clicked under pause. Mirror the same gate for the
  bidding action panel.

### UX-22: pauseOverlay does NOT EnableMouse — clicks pass to cards beneath
- Severity: MED
- Cue: pauseOverlay at UI.lua:1290 is created without
  `EnableMouse(true)` — it's a passive backdrop. The pause button
  is bumped to FULLSCREEN_DIALOG strata to keep it clickable, but
  cards below the overlay (centerCards, AKA banner content) are
  still clickable via mouse-enter. Combined with UX-21 above, the
  pause is mostly cosmetic — the underlying widgets keep firing.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1290-1294. Pause button's frame strata bump
  at 1310 is correct; the missing piece is `pauseOverlay:
  EnableMouse(true)`.
- Fix sketch: add `pauseOverlay:EnableMouse(true)` so the overlay
  swallows non-pause-button clicks within centerPad while paused.

### UX-24: Main window is not in `UISpecialFrames` — Escape doesn't close it
- Severity: MED
- Cue: WoW convention is for floating windows to register in
  `UISpecialFrames` so pressing Escape closes them in LIFO order.
  WHEREDNGN's main window lacks this. The only close affordance
  is the `UIPanelCloseButton` at top-right (UI.lua:507), and even
  THAT doesn't dismiss while a confirmation popup is open.
- Root cause: UI.lua:399 creates the frame but never appends its
  global name to `UISpecialFrames`.
- Fix sketch: `tinsert(UISpecialFrames, "WHEREDNGNFrame")` after
  the frame's creation. (Watch out: if any state machine relies
  on the frame staying open during a phase, that needs a re-show
  hook.)

### UX-25: Easter overlay click-anywhere dismiss can mask a legitimate-action click
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: Easter overlay covers UIParent at FULLSCREEN_DIALOG with
  EnableMouse(true) and OnMouseDown → Hide. A player about to
  click "Skip" during an escalation phase could click through the
  overlay (dismissing it) while the click is interpreted as a
  generic dismiss; their intended Skip was queued for the now-
  hidden button. Not catastrophic — they can re-click — but the
  5-second photo can swallow a click and not register that the
  user wanted to interact with the underlying UI.
- Root cause: Easter.lua:104 (single OnMouseDown handler).
- Fix sketch: brief 0.4s no-dismiss grace window after the
  overlay shows, so a misfire isn't conflated with intent-to-
  dismiss; or fade the overlay during the last 0.5s so the user
  sees it's about to clear.

## Banner / glow / countdown

### UX-30: Active-seat turnGlow uses the same gold tint as legal-edge cards (visual mush)
- Severity: MED
- Cue: `COL.activeGlow = {1.00, 0.84, 0.30, 0.22}` (UI.lua:170)
  is a partially-transparent overlay covering the entire seat
  badge AND the same color is used for `COL.legalEdge` on legal
  hand cards (UI.lua:169). When it's the local player's turn,
  every legal card has a gold edge AND the localBar has a gold
  glow — the player's eye lands on the bar (oversized highlight)
  rather than on the actually-actionable cards.
- Root cause: UI.lua:169-170; legalEdge on cards at 2305;
  turnGlow on localBar at 1613-1616.
- Fix sketch: use a different color for the active seat (e.g.
  warm orange) vs legal-card edges (existing gold). Or reduce
  turnGlow alpha to ~0.10 so it reads as ambient highlight, not
  attention sink.

### UX-31: AKA banner has no fade-in / fade-out — abrupt show/hide
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `renderAKABanner` simply calls `b:Show()` / `b:Hide()`.
  When AKA fires mid-trick, the banner pops in instantly,
  competing with the active card animation underneath.
- Root cause: UI.lua:3469-3490. No alpha animation.
- Fix sketch: 200ms alpha fade-in / fade-out via UIFrameFadeIn
  (Blizzard standard) keyed to akaCalled state-change.

### UX-32: Overcall banner triggers full U.Refresh once per second
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: the overcall countdown OnUpdate (UI.lua:1358-1392) calls
  `U.Refresh()` whenever `remain` changes (every full second) so
  action-button labels show the new digit. Refresh re-renders
  every seat, hand card, banner, status — heavy work for a 1-Hz
  cosmetic update.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1390. The action-button labels carry the
  countdown digit (`rTag`), which is the only thing that needs
  updating.
- Fix sketch: instead of `U.Refresh`, walk `actionPool` and
  re-set the relevant label suffix in place. Or move the
  countdown into the banner body only and drop the per-button
  digit.

### UX-33: SWA banner countdown stops at 0 but the banner stays — auto-approve transition is invisible
- Severity: MED
- Cue: SWA banner OnUpdate (UI.lua:1473-1504) clamps `remain` to
  0 via `math.max`. When the 5s window expires, the host fires
  the auto-approve, but the banner only hides on phase-change
  via `renderSWABanner`. There's no visible "AUTO-APPROVED"
  transition — banner just disappears, cards vanish, score
  banner appears. Users miss the cause-and-effect.
- Root cause: UI.lua:3427-3467 — render branch hides the banner
  when `req` is nil with no transitional state.
- Fix sketch: when `remain == 0`, swap the body text to "auto-
  approving…" for ~0.4s before clearing. Drives the same logic
  used for "decided already" in the overcall window
  (UI.lua:1962-1976).

### UX-34: Trick winner-glow has no constant — uses hardcoded 2.2s in Net.lua
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `K.TRICK_GLOW_SEC = 1.0` is defined in Constants.lua:389
  but unused. The actual hold is hardcoded `C_Timer.After(2.2,
  ...)` in Net.lua:1995. Tweaking the constant has no effect.
- Root cause: Constants.lua:389 (dead constant) vs Net.lua:1995
  (hardcoded literal).
- Fix sketch: replace the literal with `K.TRICK_GLOW_SEC` and
  update the comment to reflect the 2.2s value, OR delete the
  constant.

### UX-35: AFK pulse uses hardcoded 8 ticks × 0.18s = 1.4s — not configurable
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: U.PulseTurn at UI.lua:3626-3650 hardcodes `ticks=8,
  every=0.18`. 1.4s of red-flash on the local seat is sometimes
  too brief to notice (especially when player is mid-dialog with
  the fight). No tunable.
- Root cause: UI.lua:3632-3633.
- Fix sketch: pull to constants `K.UI_AFK_PULSE_TICKS=8,
  K.UI_AFK_PULSE_PERIOD=0.18` so future tuning lands in one
  place.

## Tooltips and error feedback

### UX-40: SWA / TAKWEESH / AKA / BALOOT action buttons have no tooltip
- Severity: HIGH
- Cue: TAKWEESH carries a 2-click confirm safety, but has no
  tooltip explaining "you're claiming the opposing team played an
  illegal card; if right, the round ends with handTotal × mult to
  YOUR team; if wrong, the same handTotal × mult goes to the
  OTHER team". For SWA the same: no tooltip explains <=3-card
  vs 4+-card permission flow. AKA: no tooltip on what the suit
  glyph means. BALOOT!: no tooltip. New players have to read
  CHANGELOG / docs to learn the consequences of the very buttons
  the addon shows them.
- Root cause: addAction / addConfirmAction at UI.lua:1672, 1695
  don't set OnEnter handlers.
- Fix sketch: thread a `tooltip` field through addAction so each
  call site can (optionally) supply 2-line copy; bind OnEnter /
  OnLeave to GameTooltip when present.

### UX-41: Ashkal / Sun / Hokm / Kawesh bid buttons have no tooltip
- Severity: HIGH
- Cue: A new player on round 1 sees "Pass / Hokm ♣ / Ashkal /
  Sun / Kawesh" — five gnomic options. Ashkal's restriction (3rd
  / 4th seat only, partner becomes declarer) is non-obvious.
  Kawesh requires 5 of {7, 8, 9}. Hokm picks the trump suit. No
  hover help.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1719-1796 — every addAction call here
  passes label + onclick only.
- Fix sketch: same as UX-40 — extend addAction's signature with
  an optional tooltip, supply Saudi-specific copy.

### UX-42: Illegal-card edge color is the same red as Takweesh-warning border
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: when a card is illegal in the active turn, its edge is
  `COL.badEdge = {0.55, 0.20, 0.20}` (UI.lua:168, 2311). The
  TAKWEESH button text is also red. The visual signal "this
  card is illegal" overlaps with "this button calls out illegal
  plays" — same color, same context, opposite meaning.
- Root cause: UI.lua:168 (badEdge), 2311.
- Fix sketch: use orange `{0.85, 0.45, 0.10}` for illegal-card
  warning so it's distinct from TAKWEESH's blood-red.

### UX-43: Sound checkbox doesn't update after slash-toggle while window is open
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `muteBtn:SetScript("OnShow", ...)` (UI.lua:487) reads
  WHEREDNGNDB.sound only when the frame is shown. If the user
  is in the lobby and types `/baloot sound`, the saved variable
  flips but the visible checkbox doesn't update until they hide
  / reshow the window.
- Root cause: UI.lua:487-490 binds OnShow only.
- Fix sketch: also re-sync inside `renderLobby` and the global
  refresh path, so toggles via slash propagate to the visible
  checkbox without a window cycle.

### UX-44: Reset / minimap-icon are the only places confirming consequences explicitly
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: Reset uses StaticPopup (UI.lua:514-542) — overkill for the
  one fully-reversible action ("clears game state and returns to
  idle"). Conversely, "Host Game" / "New Game" reset state
  silently — no popup, just a print line for the "already in a
  game" guard at UI.lua:786-787.
- Root cause: UI.lua:514-542 (popup); UI.lua:777-810 (host new
  has no popup).
- Fix sketch: invert it. Host Game while not idle → popup
  ("Discard the current game and start a new lobby?"). Reset →
  inline 2-click confirm (consistent with the rest of the
  destructive-action style).

## Resize / scale / multi-resolution

### UX-50: Hand row width 680 forces card overlap at scale 0.7
- Severity: MED
- Cue: handRow is 680 wide; 8 cards × (70 + 6) − 6 = 602 wide,
  so at scale 1.0 they fit. SetScale at 0.7 affects cards too
  (proportional shrink), but at scale 1.5 the cards expand to
  ~903 — exceeding the 740-wide main frame's hand area. Result:
  outermost cards clip past the frame edge at high scale.
- Root cause: UI.lua:400 (frame 740 wide), 1561-1562 (handRow
  680 wide), 2254 (btnW=70).
- Fix sketch: shrink card width when scale > 1.2 so total stays
  within frame bounds, OR widen the main frame proportionally
  with the scale.

### UX-51: Action panel (680 wide) overflows when many actions stack
- Severity: MED
- Cue: action buttons at 90 wide + 4 spacing → 7 max in 680px
  width. PHASE_PLAY can stack: TAKWEESH + SWA + AKA + BALOOT! +
  multiple meld declarations + Done = realistic 6-8 buttons.
  Worst case 8 × 94 − 4 = 748px — overflows the panel.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1583 (actionPanel 680 wide); addAction at
  UI.lua:1672-1693 lays out left-to-right with no wrapping.
- Fix sketch: wrap to a second row when total exceeds panel
  width, OR shrink button width dynamically when count > 6.

### UX-52: SWA banner card row hardcodes 4 slots — won't survive a future SWA-on-5+ change
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: UI.lua:1427-1436 pre-builds 4 card slots. SWA fires at
  ≤4 cards remaining (SWA_TIMEOUT_SEC convention), so 4 is right
  today. But the related Saudi rule (5+-card permission) still
  shows a 4-slot strip even though the encoded hand has 5 cards
  → only the first 4 render visibly; the 5th card is silently
  hidden.
- Root cause: UI.lua:1428 (`for i = 1, 4 do`).
- Fix sketch: dynamically grow slots to `min(handCount, 8)`
  rather than hardcoding 4.

### UX-53: Minimap icon radius 80 is hardcoded — clipping risk on small minimaps
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: MinimapIcon.lua:18 sets `RADIUS = 80`. Default Blizzard
  minimap is 140 wide → radius 80 sits OUTSIDE the rim by ~10px
  but inside the icon's natural drag arc. Players who shrink
  their minimap (common with some UI overhauls) end up with the
  icon floating in midair.
- Root cause: MinimapIcon.lua:18.
- Fix sketch: `RADIUS = (Minimap:GetWidth() / 2) + 6` computed
  per call in `positionButton`, so it tracks the rim.

## Color and semantic palette

### UX-60: Color blind: COL.txtUs (green) vs COL.txtThem (red) — no luminance contrast
- Severity: MED
- Cue: `COL.txtUs = "ff66ff88"` (saturated green) and `COL.txtThem
  = "ffff7777"` (saturated red) at UI.lua:174-175. Both have
  similar luminance; deuteranopia / protanopia (~5% of male
  players) can't distinguish team labels. The whole round-end
  banner relies on this red/green dichotomy for "we won" vs "we
  lost" headlines.
- Root cause: UI.lua:174-175.
- Fix sketch: shift txtThem toward blue/orange (e.g. `ffff8855`
  with brightened luminance) so red-green-blind players still
  see the contrast. Also pair the color with a glyph (e.g. ✓
  for win, × for loss) in the WIN/LOST headline.

### UX-61: Bid card suit colors use 4-color scheme on cards but 2-color in chat
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `K.SUIT_COLOR_ONCARD` is the 4-color deck (♠ black, ♥ red,
  ♦ blue, ♣ green) for visual distinctness on cards. But
  `K.SUIT_COLOR` (used in chat / glyphs in some seat labels) is
  a different 4-color set with reds and oranges that DON'T match
  the card-face colors. Diamond is orange in chat but blue on
  cards.
- Root cause: Constants.lua:20 vs Constants.lua:26-31.
- Fix sketch: reuse `SUIT_COLOR_ONCARD` for the chat-side glyph
  rendering as well so a "♦" everywhere reads the same blue.

### UX-62: AFK pulse red overrides legal-edge gold without restore
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: U.PulseTurn ends with "leave it on the legal-edge gold;
  the next Refresh re-derives the right color from S.s.turn
  anyway" (UI.lua:3645). But if the local player is NOT actually
  the active turn at pulse-end (the pulse fires for upcoming
  turns?? — actually it only arms when our turn → fine), this
  logic is sound. But: when paused mid-pulse (host pauses while
  the warn timer is queued), the pulse can fire on a paused
  state, briefly red-flashing the localBar in a phase the user
  CAN'T act in.
- Root cause: UI.lua:3635 — pulse doesn't check `S.s.paused`.
- Fix sketch: early-return in the OnUpdate body when paused.

## Animation and transitions

### UX-70: Card-land animation is 8 discrete steps — visibly steppy at scale > 1.2
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `animateLand` at UI.lua:2640-2664 uses 8 ticker steps
  over 0.22s = 27.5ms per step ≈ 36 fps. At scale 1.5 the cards
  travel ~210px (140 × 1.5) in 8 steps = 26px/step jumps that
  read as choppy on high-refresh monitors.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2640 hardcodes `steps = 8`.
- Fix sketch: use OnUpdate-driven smooth interpolation (compute
  position via elapsed time, not discrete steps) — scales
  perfectly to any framerate.

### UX-71: peekLastTrick has no fade — abrupt swap from live trick to last-trick override
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: pressing the "?" peek button toggles `centerOverride`
  immediately (UI.lua:2773). Cards swap content abruptly. After
  3 seconds, equally abrupt swap back.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2772-2778.
- Fix sketch: 200ms alpha fade-out → swap content → fade-in.

### UX-72: Bid card disappears instantly when phase advances to PLAY
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: `centerCards.bid` is hidden via `slot.frame:Hide()` with
  no transition when phase enters PLAY (UI.lua:2718, exits the
  bid-card branch into trick-card branch). The visual cue
  "contract is set, play starting" loses its anchor.
- Root cause: UI.lua:2693-2718.
- Fix sketch: alpha fade the bid card to 0 over 0.3s before the
  first trick card slides in.

### UX-73: Status banner "Round done: A +X, B +Y" uses raw delta with no transition from PLAY
- Severity: LOW
- Cue: when PHASE_SCORE engages, `statusFor` (UI.lua:2987-2995)
  swaps the live-turn status to "Round done: …". The banner
  appears, the status flips, the hand row clears — three abrupt

- **Confirm-action wrapper** (UI.lua:1643-1662) is a clean two-
  click safety pattern for high-stakes calls (Triple, Four,

  click-momentum mistakes (v1.0.1 fix).
- **AKA/SWA/Overcall banners self-tick** at 3 Hz independently of
  Refresh, so countdown digits stay smooth without waterfalling

  reads as "my team" without memorising.

  decks and felts; SetCardStyle / SetFeltTheme correctly re-tint
